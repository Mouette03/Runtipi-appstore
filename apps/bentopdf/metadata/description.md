BentoPDF
🇬🇧 English

BentoPDF is a self‑hosted PDF converter and viewer service that lets you turn documents, web pages, and more into clean PDFs on your own server.
It provides a simple, secure interface for generating and serving PDFs without relying on third‑party services.
BentoPDF is ideal for converting reports, web pages, forms, and internal documents into printable or shareable PDFs under your complete control.
Whether you need to automate PDF generation or offer PDF previews in your apps, BentoPDF handles it quietly in the background.
